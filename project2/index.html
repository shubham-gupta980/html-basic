Taco Trivia

When did the tacos first apper in the india ?

Tacos first appeared in India with the opening of the first Taco Bell restaurant in Bangalore in 2010. Prior to that, there were a few individual attempts at creating taco-style dishes, but Taco Bell is credited with introducing the concept on a larger scale 2011.(source : Taco Bell restaurant in Bangalore)
